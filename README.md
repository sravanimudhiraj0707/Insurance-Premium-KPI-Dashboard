# Insurance-Premium-KPI-Dashboard
# problem Statement
TruSecure CreDebit Insurance manages a complex portfolio of policies from 2015 to 2025, yet critical data on policy creation, tenure, customer details, claims, loan information, and maturity calculations remains fragmented across multiple systems. Without a unified view, stakeholders struggle to track overall business performance, analyze claim trends, or accurately forecast maturity amounts, leading to delayed and reactive decision making. The organization lacks real-time visibility into how different policy types perform across customer segments and states, making it difficult to identify top-performing plans or flag products with low uptake for timely intervention. Additionally, claim settlement performance and process inefficiencies are not actively monitored, which impacts customer satisfaction and increases operational costs. Sales, claims, and loan servicing teams operate without shared, transparent KPIs, resulting in inconsistent accountability and performance gaps across business units. Because reporting is manual, data quickly becomes outdated and stakeholders cannot access the latest insights needed for premium collection, payout planning, and risk management. To address these challenges, TruSecure requires an automated, interactive Power BI dashboard that consolidates the complete policy lifecycle into a single source of truth, enabling real-time analysis by policy type, state, and segment while driving accountability and faster strategic decisions.
# Insights
1. Executive Summary
KPI Tracking: Real-time monitoring of 7,299+ policies.
Financial Metrics: Total Annual Premium ($2,161.60M), Total Paid vs. Payable amounts, and Underwriting expenses.
Customer Detail: Granular table view showing tenure, ROI, and payment duration per policyholder.
2. Market & Policy Insights
Policy Segmentation: Analysis of Endowment, Universal, and Whole Life insurance types.
Demographics: Revenue breakdown by Occupation (e.g., Theatre, Horticulture) and State (Delhi & UP leading).
Sales Trends: Historical line charts showing premium growth from 2016 to 2024.
3. Investment & ROI Analysis
Maturity Forecasting: Visual comparison of Total Premium Paid vs. Projected Maturity Value.
ROI Performance: Tracking the Average Annualized ROI, which peaked at 19.05% in the latest fiscal year.
4. Sales Hierarchy & Performance
Leaderboard: Stacked bar charts comparing sales targets vs. actuals for agents like Biju Singh and Devij Malhotra.
Managerial View: Regional and Zonal manager performance tracking to identify leadership bottlenecks.
🛠️ Tech Stack
Power BI Desktop: Used for Data Modeling, DAX (Data Analysis Expressions), and Visualization.
Power Query: For ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes and data cleaning.
Excel/SQL: Data source management and initial data structuring.
📈 Key Business Insights
Revenue Drivers: Universal and Whole Life policies contribute nearly 68% of the total annual premium.
Geographic Focus: The Northern region (Delhi/UP) is the primary revenue generator; expansion strategies are recommended for Central regions.
Operational Efficiency: Currently, 63.82% of premiums are successfully collected, with a targeted plan needed for the 36.18% payable balance.
